Based on the methodology outlined in Chase AI's video, [The 7 Levels of Building ELITE Websites with Claude Code](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1PXFAFMgdns), this repo provides a roadmap for leveraging AI to build truly premium, customized web applications.

## 🎯 The Objective: Crafting Your Own Web Experience
Instead of following a rigid tutorial for a specific mock app, this repository is built around **the progression of your own design skills**. The goal is to take your own ideas—whether it's a personal portfolio, a SaaS landing page, or a creative project—and guide you through the process of bringing them to life.

As you navigate from Level 1 to Level 7, you will learn how to evolve a basic concept from a generic, uninspired AI layout into a highly customized, visually stunning, and interactive web experience that is entirely your own.

---

## 📂 Project Structure: The 7 Learning Levels

Each folder in this repository corresponds to a distinct phase in the AI web design learning curve. We recommend creating your own landing page and applying the concepts of each folder to see your site level up.

### `📁 level-1-the-prompt`
**The "You and a Prompt" Stage.** 
* **The Concept:** This represents relying entirely on basic text prompts and Claude's Plan Mode. 
* **The Result:** The classic "AI Slop." By exploring this level, you'll see what happens when you rely on the AI to fill in the design gaps without giving it clear taste or direction—expect generic layouts, uninspired typography, and default gradients.

### `📁 level-2-design-skills`
**Giving Claude a Design Education.** 
* **The Concept:** Bringing in outside, specialized prompts (like the open-source `UIUX Pro Max` skill) to inject color theory, typography rules, and spatial awareness into your AI workflow.
* **The Result:** You will notice a significant structural improvement. The site looks cleaner and uses better layouts, but it still inherently feels like a standard "AI-designed template." 

### `📁 level-3-visual-director`
**Showing, Not Just Telling.** 
* **The Concept:** Sourcing visual inspiration from platforms like Awwwards, Godly, Pinterest, and Dribbble. Here, you learn to feed UI screenshots directly into Claude so it has a concrete reference of what "good" looks like.
* **The Result:** A much closer match to premium design trends. However, this level teaches you about the "vibe gap" ceiling—translating pure screenshots to code can lead to endless prompting loops to fix minor layout misinterpretations.

### `📁 level-4-the-cloner`
**Learning from the Pros (Deconstruction).** 
* **The Concept:** Moving beyond surface-level images to extract the actual HTML, CSS, and JS from high-end websites. You will use custom "site teardown" skills to let Claude read the underlying code of websites you admire.
* **The Result:** A near 1:1 structural foundation that actually functions correctly. More importantly, this folder is where you start understanding *how* professional developers execute smooth animations, dynamic backgrounds, and custom effects. 

### `📁 level-5-custom-assets`
**Bringing Your Own Flavor.** 
* **The Concept:** Integrating high-quality, pre-built components (from platforms like 21st.dev or CodePen) and injecting your own custom AI-generated media (concept art, subtle AI background videos) to apply visual storytelling. 
* **The Result:** Your project finally stops feeling like a clone or a template. It takes on its own unique brand identity, art direction, and dynamic visual weight tailored to your specific idea.

### `📁 level-6-visual-tinkering`
**Stepping Outside the Terminal.** 
* **The Concept:** Exporting the current state of the site into visual canvas tools like Stitched, Paper.design, or Pencil.dev. This teaches you how to visually iterate, redesign specific layout cards (like glassmorphism effects), and fine-tune micro-interactions (loaders, tickers, and scroll animations).
* **The Result:** A polished, premium product that feels handcrafted, intentional, and ready for production. 

### `📁 level-7-the-frontier`
**The 3D Architect.** 
* **The Concept:** Exploring the realm of custom WebGL, complex shaders, and full 3D interactive experiences that blur the line between a website and a video game. 
* **The Result:** While mostly beyond the scope of simple AI generation today, this folder contains aspirational architecture and concepts to strive for as AI tools continue to evolve.
