I'm building a Half-Elf Bard. Which College should I pick if I want to be the party's face and support healer?
For a face + support healer, College of Lore is your best fit. You get Cutting Words to debuff enemies, Magical Secrets for Healing Word, and Bonus Proficiencies to fill skill gaps. Your Half-Elf Charisma bonus makes this build shine.
What stats should I prioritize?
Charisma first — powers your spells and all Persuasion checks. Then Constitution for concentration and HP. Dexterity third for AC and initiative. I can auto-fill these into your sheet right now.
